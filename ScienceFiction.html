This book, "War of The Worlds", published in 1898, is a Science-Fiction book. Science-Fiction books are an authors way to express his/her idea of the future. so basically, these types of books are ways to predict the future. The most known science-fition item that clearly expresses the author's predictions is in "Back to The Future" (picture below). The author designs it so that it takes place in 2013, where they have hoverboards, hover cars, etc. All of which, we obviously don't have at the moment. So we can tell that the author was over-shooting their predictions a little bit.
